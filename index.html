Profile

A philologist who builds,
not just teaches

I'm a motivated philologist with a deep command of the English language and hands-on experience developing educational courses and materials. My goal is to bridge my pedagogical background with instructional design — building learning experiences that are structured, engaging, and genuinely effective.

Working at Profound Community, I didn't just deliver lessons — I designed the course architecture: sequencing units, writing original tasks, adapting content for different proficiency levels, and iterating based on learner outcomes. I brought the same care to individual students at BUKI school, developing lesson plans that balanced rigor with accessibility.

For the Ukrainian in Action course, I offer something rare: native Ukrainian language competence combined with the skills to translate that knowledge into learner-friendly, culturally rich recorded materials — using modern EdTech and AI tools to make the process both efficient and high-quality.

My philology degree, ongoing TEFL/TESOL certification, and Grade Education Centre training have given me a rigorous methodological foundation. I approach every course not as a series of topics to cover, but as a learner journey to design.
